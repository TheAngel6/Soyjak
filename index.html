What is Soyjak?

Soyjak is an online forum. This is how they would describe themselves: Soyjak.party, or simply "The Party," is an imageboard dedicated to the creation, posting, and sharing of soyjaks. As the site has grown, it has expanded to encompass many other topics as well.


What does this have to do with me?

A small part of the Soyjak community has joined the territorial community, and with this, the Discord community has experienced raids and other negative experiences. This doesn't only occur on Discord; the gaming community has seen an increase in cheating among players due to soyjaks' cheating methods.


What is this website about?
